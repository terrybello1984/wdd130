History

Established in the vibrant year of 2006 by a daring trio of siblings whose hearts beat to the rhythm of adventure, River Rush Rafting Company stands as a testament to their unwavering passion for extreme sports and their fervent desire to share the thrill of outdoor escapades with families far and wide.

Driven by the belief that every individual should have the opportunity to experience the exhilaration of outdoor activities without compromising safety, the founders set out to create a haven where adrenaline junkies and nature enthusiasts alike could converge in pursuit of excitement and freedom.

With a keen focus on fostering a culture of inclusivity and overcoming boundaries, River Rush Rafting Company has dedicated itself to providing not just adrenaline-pumping experiences, but also moments of connection, empowerment, and growth. Every roaring river conquered, every rapid navigated, and every heart pounding with excitement serves as a testament to the company's commitment to breaking down barriers and instilling a sense of confidence in all who dare to embark on their adventure-filled journey.

As the company continues to evolve and expand its horizons, its mission remains steadfast: to empower individuals, families, and communities to embrace the great outdoors, conquer their fears, and discover the boundless joys that await amidst the rush of the river.
