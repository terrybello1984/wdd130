Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the rafting website is to establish an online presence for a rafting business. It aims to provide information about the business, promote its services, and attract potential customers interested in thrilling rafting experiences.

Audience

The target audience for the rafting website includes adventure enthusiasts, nature lovers, and individuals seeking exciting outdoor activities. Three scenario questions for the audience are:

  1. Are you looking for an adventurous outdoor experience?
  2. Do you enjoy activities that involve nature and water?
  3. Are you interested in exploring new and thrilling challenges?

Answering yes to these questions indicates a potential interest in the rafting services offered by the business.

Color Palette

The color palette for the rafting website is inspired by nature and adventure, reflecting the excitement of rafting experiences. The selected colors are:

  • Primary Color: #FE5F55
  • Secondary Color: #2E8B57
  • Accent1 Color: #87CEEB
  • Accent2 Color: #8B4513

You can view and modify the color palette on Coolors.co.

Typography

For the rafting website, two Google Fonts have been chosen to enhance the overall design:

  • Headings Font: Overpass
  • Body Text Font: Barlow

You can use the Google Fonts API to import these fonts into your CSS file.
